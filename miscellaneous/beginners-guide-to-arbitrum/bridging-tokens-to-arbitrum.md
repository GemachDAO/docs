# 🌉 Bridging Tokens to Arbitrum

Tokens can be bridged to [Arbitrum](https://arbitrum.foundation/) using a variety of platforms. This guide covers the official Arbitrum bridge and two popular multi-chain bridges. You can also transfer directly from centralized exchanges that support Arbitrum withdrawals (Binance, Coinbase, OKX, and others).

> **Note:** You need ETH on your source chain to pay gas fees for the bridge transaction. It is recommended to start by bridging ETH, since ETH is also used to pay transaction fees once you are on Arbitrum.

---

## Official Arbitrum Bridge (Ethereum → Arbitrum)

The official bridge at [bridge.arbitrum.io](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/) transfers assets from Ethereum mainnet to Arbitrum One.

1. Visit [bridge.arbitrum.io](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/) and connect your wallet — ensure you are on the Ethereum mainnet
2. Enter the amount of ETH you want to transfer to Arbitrum One
3. Click **Move funds to Arbitrum One**
4. Verify the amount in the confirmation screen, then click **Confirm**
5. The transaction will show as **pending** — official bridge transfers take approximately 10 minutes
6. Once confirmed, your ETH is available on Arbitrum One

> **Note:** The official Arbitrum bridge only supports Ethereum → Arbitrum transfers. For bridging from other chains, use one of the multi-chain bridges below.

---

## Multi-Chain Bridges

### Relay

[Relay](https://bridge.relaychain.com/) supports fast bridging between many chains including Ethereum, Base, Optimism, BNB Chain, and Arbitrum.

1. Visit [bridge.relaychain.com](https://bridge.relaychain.com/)
2. Click **Connect Wallet** and sign in
3. Select your source chain and token, then select Arbitrum as the destination chain
4. Enter the amount to bridge
5. Click **Bridge & Swap**
6. Confirm the approval and transaction in your wallet — you must have sufficient gas tokens on the source chain
7. Your funds should appear on Arbitrum within a few minutes

### Hop Protocol

[Hop Protocol](https://app.hop.exchange/#/send?token=ETH) is another multi-chain bridge supporting ETH and stablecoins.

1. Visit [app.hop.exchange](https://app.hop.exchange/#/send?token=ETH)
2. Click **Connect a Wallet** and sign in
3. Select the asset to bridge, the source chain, and Arbitrum as the destination
4. Enter the amount and verify the amount you will receive
5. Click **Send** and follow the wallet prompts to complete the transaction

> **Note:** You must have sufficient gas tokens on the source chain to complete any bridge transaction.
