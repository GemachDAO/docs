# 📈 What is Gemach?

Gemach is a community-driven DAO building an all-in-one DeFi super app. Our ecosystem now includes:

**GDex Pro** – a high‑performance trading terminal with token discovery, real‑time analytics, memecoin feeds, copy trading of top wallets, Hyperliquid perpetuals and a powerful cross‑chain bridge.

**Yield and lending protocols** – GLend, GVault, GFund and GLoans let you supply assets, earn yield, borrow interest‑free liquidity, invest in diversified index funds and manage vault strategies.

Gemach AI – a natural-language assistant that executes swaps, bridges, lending and staking across multiple protocols and chains, and AI agents that snipe new tokens and automate on-chain trading.

Everything in the Gemach ecosystem is self‑custodial. We connect Solana, Ethereum, BNB Chain, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Sui, Sonic, Berachain and other networks through a single interface, allowing you to trade, lend, invest and earn without surrendering control of your keys. Governed by the GMAC and GLEND tokens, the Gemach DAO is committed to building the next generation of permissionless finance.
