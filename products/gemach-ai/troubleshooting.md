# Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

**Wallet Connection Problems**

* Ensure your wallet extension is up to date
* Try refreshing the page or reconnecting your wallet
* Check that you're on the correct network

**Transaction Failures**

* Verify you have enough funds for the transaction and gas fees
* Check if the token has sufficient allowance
* Try adjusting gas settings in your wallet

**Network Congestion**

* During high network activity, transactions may take longer to confirm
* Consider using a different chain if cross-chain functionality is available
