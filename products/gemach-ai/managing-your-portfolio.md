# Managing Your Portfolio

Gemach AI provides a built‑in portfolio dashboard so you can monitor all of your wallets and tokens without leaving the app.

### Viewing Your Portfolio

1. **Open the Portfolio page:** Click **Overview** under the _Portfolio_ section in the left sidebar.
2. **Add Wallets:** Click **Add Wallet** in the top right corner and connect any EVM wallet (e.g., MetaMask), Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom), or other supported wallets such as Sui or Sonic. You can add multiple wallets to aggregate your holdings.
3. **Total Portfolio Value:** Each wallet card displays its name, address and total portfolio value converted to USD. The combined value of all wallets appears at the top of the page.
4. **Token Balances:** Below the wallet cards, a table lists your token balances across chains. You can search by token symbol or name to quickly find a specific asset. Each row shows the token name, amount and value in USD.

### Tracking Performance

* **Historical performance:** Gemach AI tracks the value of your portfolios over time. Graphs and metrics show how your holdings have changed day by day.
* **Cross‑chain view:** Assets on different networks are automatically consolidated, so you can see your net position across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, BNB Chain, Optimism, Sui, Sonic, Berachain and other supported networks.
* **Alerts:** Optional notifications can inform you when a token balance changes significantly or when the market value of your holdings crosses certain thresholds.

### Managing Wallets

* **Add or remove:** Use the **Add Wallet** button to connect new wallets at any time. Click the menu on a wallet card to rename or disconnect it.
* **Multiple wallets:** There’s no limit to how many wallets you can track. This is useful for managing separate trading accounts or monitoring cold storage balances.

By consolidating your holdings and historical performance in one place, Gemach AI’s portfolio dashboard makes it easier to understand your financial position across the Gemach ecosystem and beyond.
