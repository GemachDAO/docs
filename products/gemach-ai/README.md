# ⛓️ Gemach AI

Gemach AI is a conversational interface that lets you interact with decentralized finance protocols and blockchains using natural language. Instead of navigating complex DeFi dashboards, you can ask the AI to perform tasks like swapping tokens, bridging assets between networks, managing your portfolio, or even opening and closing advanced positions.

Key features include:

* **Natural language interactions:** Describe what you want to do in plain English and Gemach AI will interpret your intent, fetch the necessary data, and prepare transactions.
* **Multi‑protocol support:** Gemach AI currently integrates with leading protocols such as Stryke for options trading, Symbiosis for cross‑chain swaps and bridging, as well as standard transfer, swap and DeFi actions. Support for additional protocols is added regularly.
* **Cross‑chain operations:** Execute swaps, bridges and other transactions across multiple networks. Supported networks include Ethereum and EVM‑compatible chains like Arbitrum, Base, BNB Smart Chain and Optimism. Bridging functions allow you to move assets to and from other networks via Symbiosis. Additional chains such as Sui, Sonic or Berachain may be supported through native wallets.
* **Portfolio tracking:** Connect one or more wallets to monitor balances, token values and historical performance across chains. Receive notifications on significant portfolio changes and generate summaries on demand.
* **AI assistant marketplace:** Discover specialized AI agents designed to help with specific tasks — from portfolio management to advanced trading strategies — and connect them to your account.
