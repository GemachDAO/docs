# FAQ

**Q: Is Gemach AI free to use?** A: The base service is available for all users. Some premium features may require holding a minimum token balance.

**Q: Which wallets are supported?** A: Gemach AI supports major Web3 wallets including MetaMask, WalletConnect, and more.

**Q: Can I use Gemach AI on mobile?** A: Yes, Gemach AI works on mobile browsers with compatible wallet apps.

**Q: Are there transaction limits?** A: Limits depend on the protocols being accessed and your wallet balance.

**Q: How does Gemach AI protect my privacy?** A: Gemach AI only accesses the information necessary to execute your requested actions. Your private keys remain secure in your wallet.

**Q: What if my transaction is stuck or pending?** A: You can check the transaction status in your wallet or a blockchain explorer. For persistent issues, contact support.

***

For additional support, join our community channels or contact the support team directly.
