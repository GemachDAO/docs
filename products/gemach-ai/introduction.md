# Introduction

Gemach AI is a conversational DeFi assistant that lets you interact with decentralized finance protocols and blockchains through a simple chat interface. Instead of navigating complex dashboards, you can ask the AI to swap tokens, bridge assets between networks, manage your portfolio or even build custom trading agents.

Gemach AI now offers three core modules:

* **Chat & History** – Ask questions or instruct the AI to perform on‑chain actions in plain English. Every conversation is recorded in your Chat History so you can resume or review past sessions.
* **Portfolio Overview** – Connect multiple wallets to view token balances, historical performance and alerts across supported networks. Add or remove wallets as needed and monitor your net worth at a glance.
* **AI Assistants** – Discover pre‑built trading bots or create your own custom agents. Assistants can be tailored with specific protocols, strategies and risk settings to automate DeFi activities.

Key features include:

* **Natural language interactions:** Describe what you want to do in plain English and Gemach AI will interpret your intent, fetch necessary data, and prepare the appropriate transaction.
* **Multi‑protocol support:** Gemach AI integrates with leading protocols such as Stryke for options trading, Symbiosis for cross‑chain swaps and bridging, as well as standard swap and transfer functions via other DeFi protocols. Additional protocol support is added regularly.
* **Cross‑chain operations:** Execute swaps, bridges and other transactions across multiple networks. Supported chains include Ethereum and EVM‑compatible networks like Arbitrum, Base, BNB Smart Chain and Optimism, as well as non‑EVM chains such as Sui, Sonic and Berachain via native wallets.
* **Portfolio tracking and management:** Monitor balances and historical performance across all connected wallets. Receive notifications when significant portfolio changes occur.
* **AI assistants and automation:** Use pre‑built bots or build custom agents to automate trading strategies, manage LP positions or execute multi‑step DeFi workflows.

Gemach AI is designed to provide a single entry point to the Gemach ecosystem, enabling both novice and advanced users to interact with DeFi protocols efficiently and securely.
