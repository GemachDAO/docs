# Getting Started with GDex.Pro

## Getting Started with GDEX.pro

This section will guide you through the initial steps to start using GDEX.pro, from creating an account to navigating the interface and understanding the basic features.

### Creating an Account

GDEX.pro uses Web3 authentication methods, which means you don't need to create a traditional username and password. Instead, you'll connect using your cryptocurrency wallet or Web3Auth.

### Connecting Your Wallet

To access all features of GDEX.pro, you'll need to connect your cryptocurrency wallet. GDEX.pro supports multiple wallet options:

#### Using Web3Auth

Web3Auth provides a seamless authentication experience, especially for users new to cryptocurrency:

1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top-right corner of the GDEX.pro interface
2. Select Web3Auth from the connection options
3. Choose your preferred authentication method (Google, Apple, Facebook, etc.)
4. Complete the authentication process
5. Your wallet will be created/accessed automatically

#### Using Cryptocurrency Wallets

If you already have a cryptocurrency wallet, you can connect it directly

To connect using your own wallet:

1. Select a wallet based on the network you plan to use. For EVM-compatible networks like Ethereum, Base, BNB Smart Chain, Arbitrum and Optimism, use a wallet such as MetaMask or WalletConnect. For Solana, use a Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom). For the Sui network, use a Sui-compatible wallet. For emerging networks like Sonic or Berachain, use the native wallet recommended for those chains.
2. Approve the connection request in your wallet application when prompted.
3. Once approved, your wallet will be connected to GDEX.pro and you can begin using the platform.

### Navigating the Interface

Once you've connected your wallet, you'll have access to all GDEX.pro features. The main navigation menu at the top of the screen includes:

* **DISCOVERY**: Find new token pairs and opportunities
* **PORTFOLIO**: Manage your crypto assets
* **MEMESCOPE**: Access customized token feeds
* **BUBBLE MAP**: Visualize token market caps
* **BRIDGE**: Exchange crypto across blockchains
* **COPYTRADE**: Copy successful traders' strategies

#### Main Dashboard Elements

After connecting your wallet, you'll see:

* Your wallet address in the top-right corner
* Your current balance
* Navigation options for different features
* Recent activity and notifications

### Making Your First Transaction

#### Buying Cryptocurrency with Apple Pay

If you're new to cryptocurrency, you can easily purchase your first tokens using Apple Pay:

1. Navigate to the purchasing section
2. Select the cryptocurrency you wish to buy
3. Enter the amount
4. Choose Apple Pay as your payment method
5. Confirm the transaction using Face ID, Touch ID, or your passcode
6. Your purchased cryptocurrency will appear in your GDEX.pro wallet

#### Using the Bridge Feature

To exchange cryptocurrencies across different blockchains:

1. Navigate to the BRIDGE section
2. Select the source cryptocurrency (what you're sending)
3. Select the destination cryptocurrency (what you want to receive)
4. Enter the amount you wish to exchange
5. Click the "Exchange" button
6. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
7. Wait for the cross-chain transfer to complete

#### Exploring Copy Trading

To start copy trading:

1. Navigate to the COPYTRADE section
2. Browse the list of available traders
3. Review their performance metrics
4. Click the "Copy trade" button next to your chosen trader
5. Set your copy trading parameters
6. Confirm to start copying their trades

### Understanding Key Metrics

As you use GDEX.pro, you'll encounter various metrics and terms:

* **PNL**: Profit and Loss, showing your trading performance
* **Liquidity**: How easily a token can be bought or sold without affecting its price
* **Market Cap**: Total value of all tokens in circulation
* **Volume**: Amount of the token traded in a given period
* **Win Rate**: Percentage of successful trades
