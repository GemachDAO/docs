### Key Features

#### Discovery

Find new token pairs in real-time with comprehensive information about liquidity, market cap, volume, and more. The Discovery feature helps you identify potential opportunities in the ever-evolving crypto market.

#### Portfolio Management

Track all your crypto assets in one place with detailed performance metrics and transaction history. The Portfolio feature gives you a clear overview of your investments and their performance over time.

#### Memescope

Access customized real-time feeds of new tokens matching your selected preset filters. Memescope helps you stay on top of the latest trends and opportunities in the meme coin space.

#### Bubble Map

Visualize the TOP100 popular tokens by market capitalization with an interactive bubble chart. The Bubble Map provides an intuitive way to understand market dynamics and token relationships.

#### Bridge Functionality

Exchange cryptocurrencies across different blockchains seamlessly. The Bridge feature allows you to move your assets between networks like Solana and Ethereum with ease.

#### Copy Trading

Automatically replicate the trading strategies of successful traders on the platform. Copy Trading lets you leverage the expertise of top performers to potentially improve your own results.
