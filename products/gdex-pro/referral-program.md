# Referral Program

Welcome to GDEX’s Referral Program! At GDEX, we believe in rewarding our community for helping us grow. This program allows you to earn passive income when the people you invite trade on GDEX.

### Why Refer Others?

* Earn commission whenever your referrals trade. The more they trade, the more you earn.
* Help friends benefit from a reliable trading platform. Everyone wins when your network joins GDEX.

### How It Works

Our referral program has two tiers:

| Tier                         | Who Counts?                                            | Your Commission                                                           | Example                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Level 1 – Direct referrals   | Anyone who signs up using your unique referral link    | 30% of GDEX’s net trading fee from each trade your Level 1 referrals make | Person B signs up using your link and trades $10,000 in a day. Assuming GDEX’s trading fee is 1%, the fee generated from their trades is $100. Your commission: 30% of $100 = $30.     |
| Level 2 – Indirect referrals | People who sign up using your Level 1 referrals’ links | 10% of GDEX’s net trading fee from each trade your Level 2 referrals make | Person B (your Level 1 referral) invites Person C, who trades $10,000 in a day. The trading fee is $100. Your commission: 10% of $100 = $10. Person B’s commission: 30% of $100 = $30. |

#### Notes

* Commissions are based on GDEX’s net fee. This means your share is calculated after applying any discounts or promotions.
* There’s no cap on earnings. The more people you refer, and the more they trade, the more you earn.
* Your Level 1 referrals earn 30
*   Getting Your Referral Link

    1. Log in to your GDEX account.
    2. Navigate to the Referral Program section.
    3. Copy your unique referral link and share it with friends, family, or on social media.

    Whenever someone signs up using your link and starts trading, you’ll automatically receive your share of the trading fees.

    ### How Much Can You Earn?

    Your earnings depend on the trading activity of your referrals. Below is a rough illustration for two scenarios:

    | Scenario                        | Trading Volume | Net Fee (assumed 1%) | Your Commission   |
    | ------------------------------- | -------------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
    | Level 1 referral trades $10,000 | $10,000        | $100                 | $30 (30% of $100) |
    | Level 2 referral trades $10,000 | $10,000        | $100                 | $10 (10% of $100) |

    As you can see, inviting active traders can lead to significant passive income. And because Level 1 referrals earn 30% from their own direct referrals, your network has an incentive to expand—further increasing your potential earnings.
