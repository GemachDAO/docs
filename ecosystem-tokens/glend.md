# 🪙 GLEND

## **A Utility Token With Yield**

GLEND is inherently a utility token that allows users to earn from protocol revenues, participate in governance, and join our community.

## Our Sustainable Token Flywheel

* buy back tokens from the open market with protocol revenue
* the purchased tokens will go to TVL accrual incentives
* higher TVL equals higher protocol revenue&#x20;

GLend has a treasury which will be strategically invested in diversified yield strategies. The resulting revenue will contribute to our sustainable token flywheel. This revenue will also be utilized to grow the treasury, support development, marketing, and other proposals approved by token holders.
