# 🪙 GLEND

GLEND is the utility and governance token for the GLend protocol — Gemach's algorithmic lending and borrowing money markets on Arbitrum. Holding GLEND gives you a share of protocol revenue, governance rights, and access to community incentives.

---

## Token Overview

GLEND is designed around a sustainable token flywheel:

1. Protocol revenue is used to **buy back GLEND from the open market**
2. Purchased tokens are deployed as **TVL accrual incentives** — rewarding liquidity providers
3. Higher TVL generates **more protocol revenue** — completing the flywheel

This mechanism creates aligned incentives between protocol growth and token value: as GLend is used more, more revenue flows to GLEND holders.
